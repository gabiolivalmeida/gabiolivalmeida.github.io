Bubbl: a playful app for child empowerment

Research | User Flow | Wireframes | Prototypes

Bubbl is a mobile app that uses a customizable mascot, interactive games, and drawing activities to guide children through self-awareness, personal boundaries, bullying, and body safety. My contribution encompassed the entire design cycle, from in-depth research into child psychology and digital media consumption to delivering polished mockups for this highly engaging and educational mobile experience.

Beverage Order Modification Product

Research | User Flow | Wireframes | Prototypes | Usability Test

I led the design of a product enabling clients and partners to modify orders. My process involved user and stakeholder interviews, persona definition, user flow design, creating mockups, and gathering feedback through usability tests.

Homepage Redesign

UI | Graphic Design

I have been tasked with redesigning the homepage of a business consulting firm. The primary objective is to develop a modern and vibrant layout while maintaining the professionalism the company has established. To achieve this, I drew inspiration from artistic movements such as Punk art and Collage, which will help create a fresh storytelling approach for the brand.
